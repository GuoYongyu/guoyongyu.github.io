向量（矩阵）对求导运算总结。

向量（矩阵）求导

1 向量（矩阵）对元素求导

1.1 行向量对元素求导

设 yT=[y1,⋯ ,yn]\pmb{y}^T = [y_1, \cdots, y_n]y​y​​yT=[y1​,⋯,yn​] 为 nnn 维行向量，xxx 为元素，则

∂yT∂x=[∂y1∂x,⋯ ,∂yn∂x]\frac{\partial \pmb{y}^T}{\partial x} = [\frac{\partial y_1}{\partial x}, \cdots, \frac{\partial y_n}{\partial x}] ∂x∂y​y​​yT​=[∂x∂y1​​,⋯,∂x∂yn​​]

1.2 列向量对元素求导

设 y=[y1,⋯ ,ym]T\pmb{y} = [y_1, \cdots, y_m]^Ty​y​​y=[y1​,⋯,ym​]T 为 mmm 维列向量，xxx 为元素，则

∂y∂x=[∂y1∂x⋮∂ym∂x]\frac{\partial \pmb{y}}{\partial x} = \left[ \begin{matrix} \frac{\partial y_1}{\partial x} \\ \vdots \\ \frac{\partial y_m}{\partial x} \end{matrix} \right] ∂x∂y​y​​y​=⎣⎢⎡​∂x∂y1​​⋮∂x∂ym​​​⎦⎥⎤​

1.3 矩阵对元素求导

设 Y=[yij]Y = [y_{ij}]Y=[yij​] 为 m×nm\times nm×n 矩阵，xxx 为元素，则

∂Y∂x=[∂y11∂x⋯∂y1n∂x⋮⋮⋮∂ym1∂x⋯∂ymn∂x]\frac{\partial Y}{\partial x} = \left[ \begin{matrix} \frac{\partial y_{11}}{\partial x} &\cdots &\frac{\partial y_{1n}}{\partial x} \\ \vdots &\vdots &\vdots \\ \frac{\partial y_{m1}}{\partial x} &\cdots &\frac{\partial y_{mn}}{\partial x} \end{matrix} \right] ∂x∂Y​=⎣⎢⎡​∂x∂y11​​⋮∂x∂ym1​​​⋯⋮⋯​∂x∂y1n​​⋮∂x∂ymn​​​⎦⎥⎤​

2 元素对向量（矩阵）求导

2.1 元素对行向量求导

设 yyy 为元素，xT=[x1,⋯ ,xq]\pmb{x}^T = [x_1, \cdots, x_q]xxxT=[x1​,⋯,xq​] 为 qqq 维行向量，则

∂y∂xT=[∂y∂x1,⋯ ,∂y∂xq]\frac{\partial y}{\partial \pmb{x}^T} = [\frac{\partial y}{\partial x_1}, \cdots, \frac{\partial y}{\partial x_q}] ∂xxxT∂y​=[∂x1​∂y​,⋯,∂xq​∂y​]

2.2 元素对列向量求导

设 yyy 为元素，x=[x1,⋯ ,xp]T\pmb{x} = [x_1, \cdots, x_p]^Txxx=[x1​,⋯,xp​]T 为 ppp 维行向量，则

∂y∂x=[∂y∂x1⋮∂y∂xp]\frac{\partial y}{\partial \pmb{x}} = \left[ \begin{matrix} \frac{\partial y}{\partial x_1} \\ \vdots \\ \frac{\partial y}{\partial x_p} \end{matrix} \right] ∂xxx∂y​=⎣⎢⎢⎡​∂x1​∂y​⋮∂xp​∂y​​⎦⎥⎥⎤​

2.3 元素对矩阵求导

设 yyy 为元素，X=[xij]X = [x_{ij}]X=[xij​] 为 p×qp\times qp×q 矩阵，则

∂y∂X=[∂y∂x11⋯∂y∂x1q⋮⋮⋮∂y∂xp1⋯∂y∂xpq]\frac{\partial y}{\partial X} = \left[ \begin{matrix} \frac{\partial y}{\partial x_{11}} &\cdots &\frac{\partial y}{\partial x_{1q}} \\ \vdots &\vdots &\vdots \\ \frac{\partial y}{\partial x_{p1}} &\cdots &\frac{\partial y}{\partial x_{pq}} \end{matrix} \right] ∂X∂y​=⎣⎢⎢⎡​∂x11​∂y​⋮∂xp1​∂y​​⋯⋮⋯​∂x1q​∂y​⋮∂xpq​∂y​​⎦⎥⎥⎤​

3 行（列）向量对列（行）向量求导

3.1 行向量对列向量求导

设 yT=[y1,⋯ ,yn]\pmb{y}^T = [y_1, \cdots, y_n]y​y​​yT=[y1​,⋯,yn​] 为 nnn 维行向量，x=[x1,⋯ ,xp]T\pmb{x} = [x_1, \cdots, x_p]^Txxx=[x1​,⋯,xp​]T 为 ppp 维行向量，则

∂yT∂x=[∂y1∂x1⋯∂yn∂x1⋮⋮⋮∂y1∂xp⋯∂yn∂xp]\frac{\partial \pmb{y}^T}{\partial \pmb{x}} = \left[ \begin{matrix} \frac{\partial y_1}{\partial x_1} &\cdots &\frac{\partial y_n}{\partial x_1} \\ \vdots &\vdots &\vdots \\ \frac{\partial y_1}{\partial x_p} &\cdots &\frac{\partial y_n}{\partial x_p} \end{matrix} \right] ∂xxx∂y​y​​yT​=⎣⎢⎢⎡​∂x1​∂y1​​⋮∂xp​∂y1​​​⋯⋮⋯​∂x1​∂yn​​⋮∂xp​∂yn​​​⎦⎥⎥⎤​

3.2 列向量对行向量求导

设 y=[y1,⋯ ,ym]T\pmb{y} = [y_1, \cdots, y_m]^Ty​y​​y=[y1​,⋯,ym​]T 为 mmm 维列向量，xT=[x1,⋯ ,xq]\pmb{x}^T = [x_1, \cdots, x_q]xxxT=[x1​,⋯,xq​] 为 qqq 维行向量，则

∂y∂xT=[∂y1∂x1⋯∂y1∂xq⋮⋮⋮∂ym∂x1⋯∂ym∂xq]\frac{\partial \pmb{y}}{\partial \pmb{x}^T} = \left[ \begin{matrix} \frac{\partial y_1}{\partial x_1} &\cdots &\frac{\partial y_1}{\partial x_q} \\ \vdots &\vdots &\vdots \\ \frac{\partial y_m}{\partial x_1} &\cdots &\frac{\partial y_m}{\partial x_q} \end{matrix} \right] ∂xxxT∂y​y​​y​=⎣⎢⎢⎡​∂x1​∂y1​​⋮∂x1​∂ym​​​⋯⋮⋯​∂xq​∂y1​​⋮∂xq​∂ym​​​⎦⎥⎥⎤​

4 行（列）向量对行（列）向量求导

4.1 行向量对行向量求导

设 yT=[y1,⋯ ,yn]\pmb{y}^T = [y_1, \cdots, y_n]y​y​​yT=[y1​,⋯,yn​] 为 nnn 维行向量，xT=[x1,⋯ ,xq]\pmb{x}^T = [x_1, \cdots, x_q]xxxT=[x1​,⋯,xq​] 为 qqq 维行向量，则

∂yT∂xT=[∂yT∂x1,⋯ ,∂yT∂xq]\frac{\partial \pmb{y}^T}{\partial \pmb{x}^T} = [\frac{\partial \pmb{y}^T}{\partial x_1}, \cdots, \frac{\partial \pmb{y}^T}{\partial x_q}] ∂xxxT∂y​y​​yT​=[∂x1​∂y​y​​yT​,⋯,∂xq​∂y​y​​yT​]

4.2 列向量对列向量求导

设 y=[y1,⋯ ,ym]T\pmb{y} = [y_1, \cdots, y_m]^Ty​y​​y=[y1​,⋯,ym​]T 为 mmm 维列向量，x=[x1,⋯ ,xp]T\pmb{x} = [x_1, \cdots, x_p]^Txxx=[x1​,⋯,xp​]T 为 ppp 维行向量，则

∂y∂x=[∂y1∂x⋮∂ym∂x]\frac{\partial \pmb{y}}{\partial \pmb{x}} = \left[ \begin{matrix} \frac{\partial y_1}{\partial \pmb{x}} \\ \vdots \\ \frac{\partial y_m}{\partial \pmb{x}} \end{matrix} \right] ∂xxx∂y​y​​y​=⎣⎢⎡​∂xxx∂y1​​⋮∂xxx∂ym​​​⎦⎥⎤​

5 矩阵对行（列）向量求导

5.1 矩阵对行向量求导

设 Y=[yij]Y = [y_{ij}]Y=[yij​] 为 m×nm\times nm×n 矩阵，xT=[x1,⋯ ,xq]\pmb{x}^T = [x_1, \cdots, x_q]xxxT=[x1​,⋯,xq​] 为 qqq 维行向量，则

∂Y∂xT=[∂Y∂x1,⋯ ,∂Y∂xq]∈Rm×nq\frac{\partial Y}{\partial \pmb{x}^T} = [\frac{\partial Y}{\partial x_1}, \cdots, \frac{\partial Y}{\partial x_q}] \in \mathbb{R}^{m \times nq} ∂xxxT∂Y​=[∂x1​∂Y​,⋯,∂xq​∂Y​]∈Rm×nq

记 Y=[y1T,⋯ ,ymT]TY = [\pmb{y}_1^T, \cdots, \pmb{y}_m^T]^TY=[y​y​​y1T​,⋯,y​y​​ymT​]T，则可以视为多个行向量对单个行向量求导

∂Y∂xT=[∂y1T∂xT⋮∂ymT∂xT]=[∂y1T∂x1⋯∂y1T∂xq⋮⋮⋮∂ymT∂x1⋯∂ymT∂xq]∈Rm×nq\frac{\partial Y}{\partial \pmb{x}^T} = \left[ \begin{matrix} \frac{\partial \pmb{y}_1^T}{\partial \pmb{x}^T} \\ \vdots \\ \frac{\partial \pmb{y}_m^T}{\partial \pmb{x}^T} \end{matrix} \right] = \left[ \begin{matrix} \frac{\partial \pmb{y}_1^T}{\partial x_1} &\cdots &\frac{\partial \pmb{y}_1^T}{\partial x_q} \\ \vdots &\vdots &\vdots \\ \frac{\partial \pmb{y}_m^T}{\partial x_1} &\cdots &\frac{\partial \pmb{y}_m^T}{\partial x_q} \end{matrix} \right] \in \mathbb{R}^{m \times nq} ∂xxxT∂Y​=⎣⎢⎢⎡​∂xxxT∂y​y​​y1T​​⋮∂xxxT∂y​y​​ymT​​​⎦⎥⎥⎤​=⎣⎢⎢⎢⎡​∂x1​∂y​y​​y1T​​⋮∂x1​∂y​y​​ymT​​​⋯⋮⋯​∂xq​∂y​y​​y1T​​⋮∂xq​∂y​y​​ymT​​​⎦⎥⎥⎥⎤​∈Rm×nq

5.2 矩阵对列向量求导

设 Y=[yij]Y = [y_{ij}]Y=[yij​] 为 m×nm\times nm×n 矩阵，x=[x1,⋯ ,xp]T\pmb{x} = [x_1, \cdots, x_p]^Txxx=[x1​,⋯,xp​]T 为 ppp 维列向量，则

∂Y∂x=[∂y11∂x⋯∂y1n∂x⋮⋮⋮∂ym1∂x⋯∂ymn∂x]∈Rmp×n\frac{\partial Y}{\partial \pmb{x}} = \left[ \begin{matrix} \frac{\partial y_{11}}{\partial \pmb{x}} &\cdots &\frac{\partial y_{1n}}{\partial \pmb{x}} \\ \vdots &\vdots &\vdots \\ \frac{\partial y_{m1}}{\partial \pmb{x}} &\cdots &\frac{\partial y_{mn}}{\partial \pmb{x}} \end{matrix} \right] \in \mathbb{R}^{mp \times n} ∂xxx∂Y​=⎣⎢⎡​∂xxx∂y11​​⋮∂xxx∂ym1​​​⋯⋮⋯​∂xxx∂y1n​​⋮∂xxx∂ymn​​​⎦⎥⎤​∈Rmp×n

记 Y=[y1,⋯ ,yn]Y = [\pmb{y}_1, \cdots, \pmb{y}_n]Y=[y​y​​y1​,⋯,y​y​​yn​]，则可以视为多个列向量对单个列向量求导

∂Y∂xT=[∂y1∂x,⋯ ,∂yn∂x]∈Rmp×n\frac{\partial Y}{\partial \pmb{x}^T} = [\frac{\partial \pmb{y_1}}{\partial \pmb{x}}, \cdots, \frac{\partial \pmb{y_n}}{\partial \pmb{x}}] \in \mathbb{R}^{mp \times n} ∂xxxT∂Y​=[∂xxx∂y1​​y1​​​y1​​,⋯,∂xxx∂yn​​yn​​​yn​​]∈Rmp×n

6 向量（矩阵）对矩阵求导

6.1 行向量对矩阵求导

设 yT=[y1,⋯ ,yn]\pmb{y}^T = [y_1, \cdots, y_n]y​y​​yT=[y1​,⋯,yn​] 为 nnn 维行向量，X=[xij]X = [x_{ij}]X=[xij​] 为 p×qp\times qp×q 矩阵，则

∂yT∂X=[∂yT∂x11⋯∂yT∂x1q⋮⋮⋮∂yT∂xp1⋯∂yT∂xpq]∈Rp×nq\frac{\partial \pmb{y}^T}{\partial X} = \left[ \begin{matrix} \frac{\partial \pmb{y}^T}{\partial x_{11}} &\cdots &\frac{\partial \pmb{y}^T}{\partial x_{1q}} \\ \vdots &\vdots &\vdots \\ \frac{\partial \pmb{y}^T}{\partial x_{p1}} &\cdots &\frac{\partial \pmb{y}^T}{\partial x_{pq}} \end{matrix} \right] \in \mathbb{R}^{p \times nq} ∂X∂y​y​​yT​=⎣⎢⎢⎡​∂x11​∂y​y​​yT​⋮∂xp1​∂y​y​​yT​​⋯⋮⋯​∂x1q​∂y​y​​yT​⋮∂xpq​∂y​y​​yT​​⎦⎥⎥⎤​∈Rp×nq

记 X=[x1T,⋯ ,xpT]TX = [\pmb{x}_1^T, \cdots, \pmb{x}_p^T]^TX=[xxx1T​,⋯,xxxpT​]T，则可以视为单个行向量对多个行向量求导

∂yT∂X=[∂yT∂x1T⋮∂yT∂xpT]∈Rp×nq\frac{\partial \pmb{y}^T}{\partial X} = \left[ \begin{matrix} \frac{\partial \pmb{y}^T}{\partial \pmb{x}_1^T} \\ \vdots \\ \frac{\partial \pmb{y}^T}{\partial \pmb{x}_p^T} \end{matrix} \right] \in \mathbb{R}^{p \times nq} ∂X∂y​y​​yT​=⎣⎢⎢⎢⎡​∂xxx1T​∂y​y​​yT​⋮∂xxxpT​∂y​y​​yT​​⎦⎥⎥⎥⎤​∈Rp×nq

6.2 列向量对矩阵求导

设 y=[y1,⋯ ,ym]T\pmb{y} = [y_1, \cdots, y_m]^Ty​y​​y=[y1​,⋯,ym​]T 为 mmm 维列向量，X=[xij]X = [x_{ij}]X=[xij​] 为 p×qp\times qp×q 矩阵，则

∂y∂X=[∂y1∂X⋮∂ym∂X]∈Rmp×q\frac{\partial \pmb{y}}{\partial X} = \left[ \begin{matrix} \frac{\partial y_1}{\partial X} \\ \vdots \\ \frac{\partial y_m}{\partial X} \end{matrix} \right] \in \mathbb{R}^{mp \times q} ∂X∂y​y​​y​=⎣⎢⎡​∂X∂y1​​⋮∂X∂ym​​​⎦⎥⎤​∈Rmp×q

记 X=[x1,⋯ ,xq]X = [\pmb{x}_1, \cdots, \pmb{x}_q]X=[xxx1​,⋯,xxxq​]，则可以视为单个列向量对多个列向量求导

∂y∂X=[∂y∂x1,⋯ ,∂y∂xq]=[∂y1∂x1⋯∂y1∂xq⋮⋮⋮∂ym∂x1⋯∂ym∂xq]∈Rmp×q\frac{\partial \pmb{y}}{\partial X} = [\frac{\partial \pmb{y}}{\partial \pmb{x}_1}, \cdots, \frac{\partial \pmb{y}}{\partial \pmb{x}_q}] = \left[ \begin{matrix} \frac{\partial y_1}{\partial \pmb{x}_1} &\cdots &\frac{\partial y_1}{\partial \pmb{x}_q} \\ \vdots &\vdots &\vdots \\ \frac{\partial y_m}{\partial \pmb{x}_1} &\cdots &\frac{\partial y_m}{\partial \pmb{x}_q} \end{matrix} \right] \in \mathbb{R}^{mp \times q} ∂X∂y​y​​y​=[∂xxx1​∂y​y​​y​,⋯,∂xxxq​∂y​y​​y​]=⎣⎢⎢⎡​∂xxx1​∂y1​​⋮∂xxx1​∂ym​​​⋯⋮⋯​∂xxxq​∂y1​​⋮∂xxxq​∂ym​​​⎦⎥⎥⎤​∈Rmp×q

6.3 矩阵对矩阵求导

设 Y=[yij]Y = [y_{ij}]Y=[yij​] 为 m×nm\times nm×n 矩阵，X=[xij]X = [x_{ij}]X=[xij​] 为 p×qp\times qp×q 矩阵，则

∂Y∂X=[∂y1T∂x1⋯∂y1T∂xq⋮⋮⋮∂ymT∂x1⋯∂ymT∂xq]∈Rmp×nq\frac{\partial Y}{\partial X} = \left[ \begin{matrix} \frac{\partial \pmb{y}_1^T}{\partial \pmb{x}_1} &\cdots &\frac{\partial \pmb{y}_1^T}{\partial \pmb{x}_q} \\ \vdots &\vdots &\vdots \\ \frac{\partial \pmb{y}_m^T}{\partial \pmb{x}_1} &\cdots &\frac{\partial \pmb{y}_m^T}{\partial \pmb{x}_q} \end{matrix} \right] \in \mathbb{R}^{mp \times nq} ∂X∂Y​=⎣⎢⎢⎢⎡​∂xxx1​∂y​y​​y1T​​⋮∂xxx1​∂y​y​​ymT​​​⋯⋮⋯​∂xxxq​∂y​y​​y1T​​⋮∂xxxq​∂y​y​​ymT​​​⎦⎥⎥⎥⎤​∈Rmp×nq
